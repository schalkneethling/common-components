Charlie Robbins

Charlie Robbins is the virtuosic guitarist and primary creative mind behind the instrumental progressive metal project Syncatto. Known for his incredibly precise, technical, and dynamic playing, Robbins crafts complex, multi-layered guitar arrangements that blend intricate modern techniques like sweeping and tapping with compelling melodies and complex rhythmic structures.

Russian Circles

Russian Circles is an instrumental post-metal band from Chicago, Illinois, formed in 2005. The band is known for its slow, atmospheric sound, which combines elements of post-metal, sludge metal, and drone metal. The band's music is characterized by its use of minimalistic instrumentation and a focus on texture and atmosphere.

Clayton King

Clayton King is a guitarist and producer based in Austin, Texas, known for his technical prowess and innovative approach to heavy music. He is a member of the band So It Begins and has also toured as a guitarist for Nothing, Nowhere. King is recognized for his intricate riff writing, which draws inspiration from a diverse range of genres.

Matteo Mancuso

Matteo Mancuso is an Italian guitarist who has rapidly gained international recognition for his extraordinary technical skill and unique approach to the instrument, particularly his intricate fingerstyle technique on the electric guitar, eschewing the use of a pick. Drawing on influences from jazz, rock, and classical guitar.

Mike Dawes

Mike Dawes is an English acoustic fingerstyle guitarist renowned for his intricate technique, which allows him to simultaneously perform melody, bass, harmony, and percussive elements on a single guitar. Known for his viral arrangements of popular songs and original compositions, Dawes employs extended techniques like percussive slapping and tapping, harmonics, and hybrid picking to create a rich, multi-layered sound.

Ro Han

Based in Melbourne, Australia Rohan 'Ro Han' Stevenson has been releasing self produced instrumental guitar music under the name 'I Built The Sky' since 2012. Ranging from complex riffs, shredding guitars, catchy melodic lead work through to intricate acoustic pieces, Ro has gained worldwide attention as one to watch among the modern guitar community.
